Evenings
Helsinki nights
are long in June.

In Juhannus week the sun barely sets — which means dinner at 8pm still feels like late afternoon, and the city stays warm and awake late into the night. Helsinki's evening restaurant scene has grown into something genuinely special: Japanese bars, Nordic bistros, neighbourhood gems in Kallio and Punavuori.

These are the places worth sitting down for when the long day finally winds down.

Where to eat
Six dinner spots
✦ Curator's note
"Sake Bar & Izakaya and Café Tampopo are sister restaurants — consider doing Tampopo for a late lunch and Sake Bar for dinner the same day. The full experience."
Japanese Bar · Kruununhaka
Sake Bar & Izakaya
The older sister of Café Tampopo — a small, intimate spot in Kruununhaka dedicated to artisanal Japanese sake and the small, exquisite bar foods that pair perfectly with it. Open Tuesday to Sunday from 5pm. Moody, welcoming, the kind of place where you order one thing and then keep ordering. Rice is life.
📍 Kruununhaka · Open Tue–Sun from 17:00
