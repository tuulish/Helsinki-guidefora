On the water
Helsinki belongs
to the sea.

The city was built on the edge of the Baltic, and you feel that everywhere. In June, the light never quite goes away, and the water glitters in that particular Nordic way that doesn't translate to photographs.

The surrounding archipelago has thousands of islands, many reachable by public ferry for the price of a bus ticket.
