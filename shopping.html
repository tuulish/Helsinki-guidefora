Bookshop · Aalto Building · Keskusta
Academic Bookstore
Alvar Aalto's building, home to the best bookshop in Northern Europe. The English section is excellent — buy something to read in the park. The building's atrium and skylight alone make it worth the stop. Café Aalto is inside if you need a break between shelves. One of those places where you go when the weather is not so inviting outside.
